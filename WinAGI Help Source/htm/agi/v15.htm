v15: Joystick Button Double Click Delay

When a joystick is used with AGI, and reserved flag f8 (double-click enabled) is set, this variable determines the amount of time when two presses of either button are treated as a double click instead of two separate single clicks. See the Joystick Input topic for additional information.

Unless you are not including joystick support in your game, you should not use this variable for any other purpose.
