Resource Load Warning RW05

Picture <number> is missing its 'end-of-resource' marker and may be corrupt

The picture resource does not end with an 'end of picture' marker (byte value of 255). Although WinAGI knows to stop at the end of the resource, when AGIdraws the picture, it will continue to read data in memory beyond the end of the resource until it finds a byte of 255. This would most likely result in corrupted visual image.

Opening and saving the picture in WinAGI will correct this anomaly.
