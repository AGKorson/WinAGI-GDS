Logic Compiler Warning 5095

Commands after a new.room or new.room.v command are always ignored by AGI

As soon as AGI encounters a new.room or new.room.v command, it stops processing the current logic, resets AGI for the new room, and then starts a new interpreter cycle. Commands that follow a new.room command will never get executed and should be removed, as they just take up space and serve no function.
