Logic Compiler Warning 5099

INPUTLINE and/or STATUSLINE > 24 will result in undesirable effects on graphics screen

If a value greater than 24 is used for INPUTLINE or STATUSLINE for the configure.screen command, the MSDOS text drawing functions used in AGI will not work properly, resulting in unpredictable effects such as blanking out areas of the screen.
