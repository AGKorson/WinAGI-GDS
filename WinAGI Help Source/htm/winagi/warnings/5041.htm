Logic Compiler Warning 5041

Setting the horizon less than 16 is unusual

The 3D aspect of AGI games is limited by the low resolution of the screen. Using a horizon value < 16 is unusual, as objects such as ego that move that high up the screen (low value of y) will not give a realistic appearance of moving into the distance. You should test your room to be sure objects behave as expected as they move toward the horizon.

Note that AGI automatically resets the horizon to 36 every time a new room is loaded. See the Horizon topic for additional information.
