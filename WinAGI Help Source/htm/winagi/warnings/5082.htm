Logic Compiler Warning 5082

'object.on.land', 'object.on.water' and 'object.on.anything' have no effect on ego object

Normally, object.on.water and object.on.land are used to restrict a screen object to just water or just land, respectively. Although you can use these commands on ego, they have no practical effect, because at the end of each interpreter cycle, AGI resets the object flags for ego that limit objects to water or land only. Using the object.on.anything command on ego has no effect for the same reason, since ego is always allowed on anything due to the forced reset of the water and land flags.
