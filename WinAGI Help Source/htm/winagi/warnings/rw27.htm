Resource Load Warning RW27

Multiple group 1 words

This warning indicates there are more than one words in group 1. Group 1 is a reserved group that tells the interpreter to automatically match a word when parsing input. There is no practical reason to have more than one because the actual word text will never be matched in AGI.

The extra words do not cause any errors, but they should be removed, as they serve no purpose and waste space in the file.
